# Little Orphan Annie Decoder

From 1935-1940, the Little Orphan Annie radio show included secret codes in its broadcasts. Anyone with that year's badge decoder could identify the secret phrase. How cool!?

_A Christmas Story_ made the decoder—and its sponsor—famous. So, `BE SURE TO DRINK YOUR OVALTINE` and use this tool to decode or encode from any badge year.

## [Try it out](https://adamd.github.io/orphan-annie-decoder/)

[Go to the decoder/encoder](https://adamd.github.io/orphan-annie-decoder/) and try one of these messages:

* `2 1 - 18 19 15 1 - 20 14 - 6 15 8 13 11 - 22 14 19 15 - 14 21 25 9 20 8 13 1` (1940, B-2)
* `20 7 - 8 14 4 7 - 12 23 - 15 14 12 - 7 9 7 4 22 - 3 7 9 7 21 23 15 7 4 - 13 6 4 8 12` (1935, D-3)
